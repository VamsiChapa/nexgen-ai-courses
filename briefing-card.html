⚡ Golden Rules
Study each day's material before teaching it. One day ahead minimum.
Open the Playbook before every class. Use the hook word-for-word.
Fist-to-Five after every concept. Re-teach if 30%+ show 0–2 fingers.
"I'll verify and come back" beats a confident wrong answer.
Use local analogies first, technical term second — never reverse.
Print code on paper as backup. Power cuts happen.
